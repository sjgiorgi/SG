CONTACT 		Discrete kth Cost Cumulant Control

ABSTRACT:
We consider a linear, time varying, discrete stochastic system with quadratic cost function. A linear feedback law is developed which minimizes a weighted sum of k cumulants of the cost function. We show that in the special case of k=1, this reduces to the classical LQG result.
