Figure: Wind and Solar power systems

This project was done with Julio Bravo.

ABSTRACT:
We consider a hybrid wind/solar/battery power system with the goal of generating and controlling power to meet a specified power demand. Each subsystem is modeled independently and then their separate power outputs are combined. The systems are combined in such as way that the wind system is considered the primary source and the solar system and battery bank are secondary. We mathematically model wind and solar power generation systems and verify the power generation with existing literature.
