CONTACT 		Statistical Model Predictive Control

DETAILS FORTHCOMING (Project in Progress)

ABSTRACT:
We formulate a statistical model predictive controller. This controller extends a typical stochastic model predictive controller by minimizing high order cumulants, instead of the standard practice which only considers the mean value of a cost function. In the unconstrained case the problem is reformulated as a semidefinite optimization problem (SDP). Next, a tight second-order cone (SOCP) approximation to the SDP is considered that can be solved in the presence of constraints.
