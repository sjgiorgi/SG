CONTACT 		Optical Fiber Cutting Robot

This robot was made during a summer internship at Current Designs in Philadelphia, PA. The robot pulls an optical fiber from a spool to a desired length, scores and breaks the fiber, and the polishes both ends. Due to time constraints, only a first prototype was made for the pulling and cutting parts. The robot was controlled via an Arduino Mega and received input from the user via a Python GUI. Both codes are listed below.

The electrical and software work was done by myself and the mechanical design was done by Chris Broughton.
